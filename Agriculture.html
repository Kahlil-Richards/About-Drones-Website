DRONES & AGRICULTURE



The agriculture industry grasped a little late on the advantage of using drones.


For example, drones can be used to fly over fields and record or capture pictures which could create a crop health map that identifies certain areas in need. With this analysis, the grower/farmer can then take action toward developing the areas of the field. Farmers also use drones to spray the land. It saves energy and time, meaning, the drone can be program to fertilize a certain area, or analyze where need fertilizing.
