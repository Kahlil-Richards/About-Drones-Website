DRONES & SCIENCE





With science, some things cannot be viewed or be determined by the human. This one aspect where drones takes play. Drones are mostly used in science to discover, determine, measure or simply record and take pictures. Drones show promise to help with a large number of societal and environmental problems.

DISASTER RESPONSE

UAVs can also help respond to disasters. Predicting of floods requires extensive data. For rural and less developed areas, the setup to measure rivers and weather for calculation is expensive. UAVs can increase measurements to deliver Intel to improve.

AREAS DRONES ARE USED
  • Capturing the spread of algae
  • Volcanic eruptions – surveillance, tracking & monitoring ash clouds
  • Mapping of excavation sites
  • Flying over historical buildings and sites
  • Climate observation
  • Surveillance of sea mammals
  • Mapping the movement of sandbars
  • Agriculture – mapping plant growth and issues, moisture levels, yields
