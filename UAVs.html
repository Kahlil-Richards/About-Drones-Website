WHAT ARE UAVS?

UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) are aircrafts that are maneuver without a pilot onboard. These aircrafts can be controlled from the ground by the use of GPS Tracking. Some drones are equipped with cameras use for recording purposes of taking pictures. Flying drones is consider as a hobby but professionals use this equipment in movie production, video shoot, etc. There are a number of drones produced but they are mainly categorize in two types:



  • Reconnaissance Uses
    - This is basically providing Battlefield intellegence.


  • Surveillance Purposes
    - This is about maintaining or keeping a track of events in its environment.
